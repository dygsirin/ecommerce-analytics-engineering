# Objective:
Understand the drivers of revenue growth, customer retention, and product performance.


# Key Business Questions

## 1. Revenue Performance

### Questions:

How has revenue changed over time?
Which product categories generate the most revenue?
Which brands contribute most to sales?
Which products drive revenue growth?

### Metrics:

Revenue
Orders
Average Order Value (AOV)


## 2. Customer Acquisition

### Questions:

Which traffic sources acquire the most customers?
Which traffic sources generate the most revenue?
What is the customer acquisition trend over time?

### Metrics:

New Customers
Revenue by Traffic Source
Orders by Traffic Source


## 3. Customer Retention

### Questions:

What percentage of customers return?
How long until a customer places a second order?
Which customer segments are most valuable?

### Metrics:

Repeat Purchase Rate
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Retention Rate


## 4. Product Performance

### Questions:

Which products are top sellers?
Which categories have the highest return rates?
Which products generate high revenue but low volume?

### Metrics:

Units Sold
Revenue
Return Rate
